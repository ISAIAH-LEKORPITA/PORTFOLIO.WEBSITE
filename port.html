About Me

A little about my journey

I'm Isaiah, a web developer and designer based in Samburu. Over the past five years I've helped startups and small studios turn rough ideas into fast, accessible websites — from the first sketch to the final line of code.

I care about clean typography, considered motion, and interfaces that get out of the user's way. When I'm not building, I'm usually sketching new layout ideas or contributing to open-source design tools.
